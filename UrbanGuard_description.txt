UrbanGuard – Mo ta chi tiet he thong

1. Tong quan he thong
UrbanGuard la he thong ban do so cho phep nguoi dan bao cao su co giao thong, xac minh thong tin bang cong dong va hien thi tren ban do.

2. Vai tro nguoi dung
- User: gui bao cao, xem ban do, xac nhan
- Admin: quan ly du lieu, kiem duyet
- System: xu ly du lieu, danh gia do tin cay

3. Chuc nang chi tiet

3.1 Dang ky / Dang nhap
- Tao tai khoan, dang nhap
Rui ro:
- spam, brute force
Giai phap:
- captcha, OTP

3.2 Gui bao cao su co
- Anh, GPS, mo ta
Rui ro:
- fake data, spam
Giai phap:
- AI, limit request

3.3 Xem ban do
- Marker, filter
Rui ro:
- lag, du lieu cu
Giai phap:
- pagination, cache

3.4 Xac nhan cong dong
- vote dung/sai
Rui ro:
- vote ao
Giai phap:
- reputation system

3.5 Trust score
- AI + user + cong dong
Rui ro:
- thao tung du lieu

3.6 AI xu ly
- nhan dien anh
Rui ro:
- sai ket qua

3.7 Upload anh
Rui ro:
- file doc hai
Giai phap:
- validate, limit size

3.8 Admin
- duyet, xoa, khoa user

3.9 Canh bao
- canh bao khu vuc nguy hiem

3.10 Database
- luu user, report
Rui ro:
- mat du lieu
Giai phap:
- backup
